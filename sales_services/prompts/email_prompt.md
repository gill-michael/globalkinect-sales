## EMAIL REQUIREMENTS

**Structure:**
1. **Subject line** — specific, curious, not salesy. Under 60 chars. No emojis. No ALL CAPS. No "Quick question" clichés.
2. **Opening line** — reference something SPECIFIC from the research report (a recent hire, a project, their LinkedIn tenure, a public statement, company news). Don't use "I hope this finds you well" or similar filler.
3. **Bridge** — in ONE sentence, connect the observed signal to a payroll / HR / compliance pain relevant to their role and company profile.
4. **Value statement** — one short sentence on what Global Kinect does, phrased around THEIR pain. **Lead with the specific service recommended in section 6 of the report** (Bureau, Managed, EOR, Immigration, or Contractor) — not generic "we run payroll".
5. **Proof point** — one specific, concrete credibility line (countries covered, Saudi data residency, UK-registered specialist) — NOT generic "we help companies save time" fluff.
6. **Ask** — 20-minute call, specific and low-commitment. Offer two concrete day options (e.g. "Tuesday or Thursday next week") or suggest they pick.
7. **Sign-off** — `[YOUR NAME]` (leave as placeholder) + "Global Kinect" + the relevant URL.

**URL choice:**
- Use **globalkinect.ae** if the prospect's company is HQ'd in or primarily operates in MENA.
- Use **globalkinect.co.uk** if the prospect's company is UK or Ireland HQ'd with MENA operations.

**Tone:**
- Direct, peer-to-peer, written for a senior professional
- Confident without being pushy
- Match the persona from the research report:
  - CFOs / Finance Directors → cost / compliance / reconciliation language
  - CHROs / HR Directors → process / experience / query-reduction language
  - Founders / Chairmen → control / visibility / operational-grip language
- British English spelling
- No buzzwords ("synergy", "leverage", "transform", "journey", "unlock", "seamless")
- No exclamation marks

**Length:**
- 120-180 words for the body
- Scannable in 15 seconds
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max each)

**Critical rules:**
- NEVER use the name "Entomo"
- Write Global Kinect as two words, never "GlobalKinect"
- Do NOT use product names "Insight", "Control", or "Orchestrate" — these are internal product codenames, not client-facing. Lead with the service name (Bureau, Managed, EOR, Immigration, Contractor).
- Do NOT mention any partner network — Global Kinect presents as covering the countries directly.
- If the research report flagged red flags in section 8, write the email but add a line at the top in square brackets: `[CAUTION: <red flag>]` — this warns the rep before they send.
- If the prospect's email is "(none on file)" in the report, still write the email — the rep will send via LinkedIn InMail using the same body.
