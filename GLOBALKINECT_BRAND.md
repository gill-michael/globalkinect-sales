## What GlobalKinect is

GlobalKinect is a payroll, HRIS, and EOR platform. One platform that replaces the mess of spreadsheets and multiple service providers that businesses currently use to manage their workforce across countries.

Clients use one login, make one submission, and get one clean view of their entire workforce — however many countries they operate in.

---

## The core message

> "Replace your payroll spreadsheets and fragmented service providers with one clean platform."

This is the foundation of every piece of copy, every headline, every CTA, every outreach message, every piece of content. The customer is escaping complexity. GlobalKinect is the clean alternative.

Supporting line:
> "One login. One submission. One view of your entire workforce across 100+ countries."

---

## The three platform modules

Clients can use one, two, or all three. Platform access (self-serve) or fully managed — their choice.

**HRIS**
Employee records, contracts, onboarding, offboarding, and workforce data. Any country. Always current. The foundation everything else runs on. Replaces scattered spreadsheets and disconnected HR records.

**Payroll Bureau**
Fully managed, fully compliant payroll across 100+ countries. One submission. We handle compliance, calculation, and output — every country, every pay cycle. Replaces multiple bureau relationships and manual monthly spreadsheet submissions.

**EOR (Employer of Record)**
Hire compliantly in any country without setting up a local entity. GlobalKinect becomes the legal employer. The client manages the work. Fully compliant in every country.

**How to describe the combination:**
"Use one module, two, or all three. Self-serve through the platform or fully managed — we handle the submissions and you get the output. The complexity disappears either way."

## Country coverage

GlobalKinect covers **100+ countries**.

State this confidently and without qualification. Never reference how coverage is delivered. Never mention:
- Partner networks
- Local bureaus
- Third-party providers
- In-house engines
- Native engines
- Delivery mechanisms of any kind

This is an internal operational detail. It does not exist in any client-facing output. Ever.

The platform experience is identical regardless of which country a client operates in.

---

## Managed service vs platform

Some clients want self-serve platform access. Some want fully managed. Most want both — managed delivery with platform visibility on top. GlobalKinect supports all three.

Never let the platform messaging make a managed service buyer feel like they're in the wrong place. Where relevant, include:
> "Want this fully managed? We handle everything — you get the output and the visibility without the admin."

This is a footnote, not a headline. The platform is always the hero. Managed service is the answer to one question, not a separate product.

## Brand rules — non-negotiable

**One word**
GlobalKinect. Never "Global Kinect" with a space. Fix every instance in every file.

**Primary CTA**
"Book a Demo" — href="/contact?intent=demo"
Every page drives toward one action: a 20-minute live platform demo. "Get a Quote" is not the goal. Secondary CTAs are fine where contextually appropriate. "Book a Demo" is always primary.

**Platform first**
GlobalKinect is always a platform. Never a bureau, never a services firm, never a consultancy. Services are delivered through the platform.

**100+ countries, always**
Never "30+ countries". Never a regional list as the headline claim. Never qualified with caveats about coverage depth.

**No partner references**
Zero mentions of partner networks, delivery infrastructure, or third-party providers in any client-facing context. Website, outreach, proposals, demos, social content, meta descriptions, schema, alt text, code comments — nowhere.

**No EOR-first positioning**
GlobalKinect is not primarily an EOR company. EOR is one of three modules. Never lead with EOR as the brand identity.

## Tone of voice

Direct. Confident. Clean.

- Short sentences.
- Specific claims over vague promises.
- No corporate padding.
- No "leverage", "solutions", "synergies", "best-in-class", "cutting-edge".
- Write like a senior operator talking to a peer — not a marketer talking at a prospect.
- If a sentence could appear on any HR software website, rewrite it until it could only appear on GlobalKinect's.

**Active, not passive**
- Not: "Payroll complexity is reduced by our platform"
- Yes: "One submission. We handle the rest."

**Specific, not vague**
- Not: "Stay compliant with local regulations"
- Yes: "Saudi EOSB calculated automatically. UAE WPS file generated on every run."

**Human, not robotic**
- Not: "GlobalKinect provides a comprehensive workforce management solution"
- Yes: "Stop sending spreadsheets to five different providers. One platform does all of it."

## Target buyers

**Segment A — GCC-based HR and Finance leaders**
Already operating in the region. Running payroll through fragmented bureaus. Feeling the pain every month. Shortest sales cycle.
- Titles: HR Director, Finance Director, Chief People Officer, Head of Payroll
- Size: 50–500 employees, 2+ countries
- Pain: No single view, different process per country, EOSB/WPS compliance risk
- Hook: "Stop managing five different bureaus. One platform, one submission, every country."

**Segment B — UK and EU companies with international workforces**
Expanding into GCC, already there, or managing distributed teams across multiple countries. High compliance anxiety. Highest deal value.
- Titles: HR Director, People Director, CFO, COO, Global Mobility lead
- Size: 100–2,000 employees globally
- Pain: Don't know GCC rules, entity risk, multi-country complexity
- Hook: "Expanding into new markets? Compliant payroll from day one — without the spreadsheets."
- Trigger: Saudi RHQ Programme — companies required to establish Riyadh HQ and run KSA payroll for the first time

**Segment C — GCC companies scaling headcount**
Saudi or UAE-headquartered, growing fast. Primary channel is the recruitment director partner.
- Titles: CEO, COO, HR Manager
- Size: 50–300 employees, growing fast
- Pain: Payroll not scaling with growth, no HRIS, manual everything
- Hook: "You're growing fast. Your payroll setup isn't. Fix that before it becomes a problem."

## Demo — the primary conversion event

Every marketing and sales touchpoint leads to one place: a 20-minute live demo.

The demo does three things:
1. Shows the platform running live payroll across the prospect's actual countries
2. Mirrors their situation — real scenario, not a generic walkthrough
3. Creates a specific next step — not "any questions?" but "can we scope what this looks like for your team?"

Demo CTA copy options:
- "Book a Demo"
- "See it running in 20 minutes"
- "See the platform live"
- "Book a 20-minute walkthrough"

Never: "Get a Quote", "Request Information", "Learn More" as a primary action.

## SEO and AEO requirements

**Every page must have:**
- Unique meta title: "[Page topic] | GlobalKinect"
- Unique meta description: 140–160 characters, includes core value proposition
- At least one structured data schema (FAQ, Service, or BreadcrumbList)
- Open Graph tags: og:title, og:description, og:type

**AEO answer blocks:**
Every content page needs a 2–3 sentence plain English answer near the top of the main content — answering the most likely question a visitor would ask about that page. These are what AI answer engines extract and cite. Keep them factual, specific, and jargon-free.

**sitemap.xml and robots.txt must exist and be current.**
